Instructions

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys: Drive your battle car (Accelerate, Steer, Reverse)
  • Left Click: Shoot energy bullets

Gameplay

  • Shoot opponents to push them out of the circular arena suspended in the sky.
  • Bullets deliver knockback force. Collect glowing Powerups to increase your bullet impact force.
  • Each player is assigned a random car with unique decals and styling.
  • The last car remaining on the platform wins!
